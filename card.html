Black Panther

They regularly hunt adult caimans (the central and south american crocidile).

  • Scientific Name: Panthera onca (America's Jaguar)
  • Weight Range: 56–96 kg (124–211 lb)
  • Length Range: 1.12 to 1.85 m (3.7 to 6.1 ft)
  • Habitat: From Mexico to South America

The adult jaguar is an apex predator, meaning it exists at the top of its food chain. It is an opportunistic hunter and its diet encompasses at least 87 species. The jaguar can take virtually any terrestrial or riparian vertebrate found in Central or South America.
